# ElandsWeather Support

ElandsWeather is a hyperlocal weather app for Elands, NSW. It aggregates readings from a personal weather station on-property with nearby BOM, OzForecast, Weather Underground and Open-Meteo sources, then learns the local microclimate over time to correct forecasts toward what actually happens at Elands.

## Privacy

ElandsWeather does not collect personal data. Weather observations from your device are stored on-device and, with your iCloud account, mirrored to a private CloudKit container for community averaging. No third-party analytics, no advertising.
